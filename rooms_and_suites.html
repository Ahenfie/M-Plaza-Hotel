Rooms & Suites

MPlaza exhibits the essence of contemporary style and luxury. Each spacious, individually decorated guestroom and suite offers a rewarding union of timeless elegance and up-to-the-moment convenience; luxurious comfort and classic sophistication. There are a total of 100 guestrooms. We have translated our unique concept of guest privacy and security into the physical design, layout and types of rooms and suites to inspire guest confidence in what we pledge. We operate the split front office system with reservations, check-in,check-out and concierge each located on different floors to give guests maximum privacy, security and convenience. With this system, it has been possible to give every guest priority attention.
