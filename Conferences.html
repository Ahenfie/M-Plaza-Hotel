M Plaza Hotel
Conferences

Hosting events at the M Plaza, particularly conferences, meetings, workshops, seminars and training is a sheer delight and void of hassle. Not only is there an inexhaustible supply of equipment, there is also good chemistry and synergy between the Event Planning and Co-ordination Team and the In-house IT Support Services Team. A simple telephone call, email or fax immediately brings into play the services of the above in a one-stop package to have everything laid on for a memorable event . Available for conferences are three halls with their capacities ranging from 50 to 250 plus syndicate rooms, a secretariat, an Internet cafe and equipped with a state-of-the-art audio-visual equipment manned by professional staff. For banqueting are the 140-seater indoor banquet hall, the 200 capacity pool terrance 1000 capacity Pace and the Plaza Court . Apart from the hosting of events in the hotel's conference and banquet halls, the M Plaza with a large fleet of vehicles provides transfer serves via mini buses and luxury 40-seater coaches for delegates attending conferences elsewhere in the city especially Accra International Conference Centre, National Theatre, etc.
